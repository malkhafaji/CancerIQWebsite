About Us

Cancer IQ is a venture-backed data analytics company with the mission to close gaps in cancer care. The concept was conceived by Dr. Olufunmilayo Olopade, a Macarthur Foundation fellow and award-winning cancer geneticist, and has since been embraced by leading technologists, healthcare professionals, and hospital systems across the country.

Our applications are designed to transform your everyday doctor into a specialist - by providing them with the knowledge and tools to deliver premium services at lower costs and with less administrative burden. Our goal is to make pro-active cancer care easy, and increase access for patients in all clinical settings.

About Us

In 2006, the big data platform used by CancerIQ today was developed at the University of Chicago by an interdisciplinary team of researchers. This effort was led by Dr. Olufunmilayo Olopade, a MacArthur Foundation Fellow award-winning cancer geneticist, and by Dr. Ian Foster, Director of the Computational Institute and the "father" of grid computing.
